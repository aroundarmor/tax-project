ITR Filing Guide for FY 2024-25

⏰ Important Deadline: File your ITR by July 31, 2026 to avoid penalties and late fees.

Which ITR Form Should You Use?

Recommended ITR Form
ITR-1 (Sahaj)

For individuals with salary income, one house property, and other income up to ₹50 lakh.

📋 Note: With Budget 2025-26 changes, more taxpayers under new regime may use ITR-1 for simpler filings.
ITR-1 (Sahaj)
For salary, one house property, other income (excluding lottery). Total income up to ₹50 lakh.
ITR-2
For individuals/HUFs not having business income. Includes capital gains, multiple properties.
ITR-3
For individuals/HUFs having business or professional income.
ITR-4 (Sugam)
For presumptive income from business/profession under sections 44AD, 44ADA, 44AE.

Step-by-Step Filing Process

1. Gather Documents: Form 16, Form 26AS, bank statements, investment proofs, TDS certificates
2. Register/Login: Visit incometax.gov.in and login with PAN
3. Download Pre-filled Data: Check Form 26AS and AIS for pre-filled information
4. Fill ITR Form: Enter income, deductions, and tax details accurately
5. Verify Tax Calculation: Ensure tax computed matches your calculations
6. E-Verify: Verify using Aadhaar OTP, net banking, or send signed ITR-V within 120 days
