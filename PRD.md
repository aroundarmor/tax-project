# TaxSaver — Product Requirements Document

## Product Vision

A SaaS tax planning platform for Indian individuals. Free tier demonstrates value; premium tier solves the whole problem.

## Target Users

- Salaried employees filing ITR for the first time
- Investors with capital gains
- Senior citizens on pension

## Monetization

- **Free**: Tax calculation, regime comparison, basic suggestions
- **Premium (₹999/year)**: Document auto-fill, AI insights, CA queue access, detailed PDF

## Pages

1. **Landing** (`index.html`) — Marketing, pricing, conversion
2. **Calculator** (`app.html`) — Free tool with upgrade nudges
3. **Premium Dashboard** (`premium.html`) — Upload docs, get AI advice
4. **Pricing** (`pricing.html`) — Plan comparison

## Premium Features

- Upload Form 16, AIS, Form 26AS, Interest Certificates
- Auto-fill calculator from uploaded documents
- AI-generated tax optimization report
- Personalized investment roadmap
- Detailed PDF with CA-grade commentary
- Priority support + CA review

## Conversion Nudges (Freemium UX)

- Blurred AI insights on calculator with "Unlock" overlay
- "You could save ₹X more" banner based on live calculation
- Only 2/5 suggestions shown free → locked card with CTA
- Social proof numbers in header

## Documents Supported

- Form 16 (Part A & B)
- AIS (Annual Information Statement)
- Form 26AS
- Bank interest certificates
- Fixed deposit interest statements
